I hade a pretty good base to start with today, the switch mounts were all in place, and all I had to do was
fuse together the plate, make a bottom and shell for the case, and boom id be done.
Originally, I had thought of making a topmounted plate, meaning that the keys wouldnt have any thing sorrounding them and would
just be sticking up from the keyboard, and its really easy

However, I just settled on making a shell that would make it look really good, and add more layers and hopefully stability to 
the keyboard.

I wanted to first make the outline of the shell, so i extruded cut out a hole from a part with the bottom of the pcb, split
and deleted all of the pillars made from the holes in the pcb, and had a indented block in which my pcb would fit.

As i was deciding how to do this, I gained inspo from my keyboard that has points around the keyboard where the shell can sit on top of
and that makes it look nice/
