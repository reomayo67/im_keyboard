I also added a copper fill zone on the ground net which resulted in some interesting things to happen.

So i was confused why this was happening, tried routing vias to somehow fill the big gaps left. Bu then i realized, its ground, so where the electricity would be disipated. electricity just couldnt get to those sealed of portions. Now this keyboard doesnt have any reliance on stable electric noise, which is what filling is good for, so i just decided to leave it cuz whats the worst that could happen.
